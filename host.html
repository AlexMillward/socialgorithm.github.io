We provide coding workshops & competitions for students, along with experienced mentors to guide them. Below are the types of events/workshops we organise.

Get in touch if you're considering hosting one.

Ultimate TTT

Ultimate TicTacToe is a game of tic tac toe on steroids. Originally popular in Mathematics circles, it involves playing nine games of tic-tac-toe on a single board, simultaneously!

Students write AI algorithms to compete against the algorithms of other students, in any programming language, following our guided workshop about Monte Carlo tree search and other goodies.
